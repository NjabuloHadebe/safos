Establish Connections

To establish contact among researchers in the field of folklore, reflecting the transition from oral to written transmission. Emphasis will be given to work in folklore from oral countries of the world and SADC countries.

Disseminate Knowledge

To accumulate, correlate, and disseminate information on folklore among scholars by means of a journal, a supplement to the journal, and a newsletter.

Seek Affiliation

To seek affiliation with other interested persons and institutions, both national and international, to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Foster Relations

To foster working relations with other like-minded organizations to advance the study and preservation of folklore traditions globally.
